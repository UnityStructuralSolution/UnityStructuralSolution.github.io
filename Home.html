About Us

Engineering with Integrity

Unity Structural Solution is a professional engineering consultancy specializing in RCC and Steel Structure design, detailing, estimation, and project support. We are two civil engineers with a combined 22+ years of industry experience, dedicated to delivering safe, economical, and practical solutions.

Our expertise spans from low-rise RCC buildings to complex steel structures, PEB engineering, and Tekla Structures detailing. We believe every project deserves accuracy, clarity, and reliability.

  • Accurate Design & Detailing
  • Reliable Estimation & BOQ
  • International Engineering Standards
  • Timely Project Delivery
What We Offer

Our Structural Services

Comprehensive engineering solutions from concept to construction documentation.

RCC Structural Design

Low-rise to mid-rise RCC buildings using ETABS, STAAD.Pro & manual verification.

RCC Structural Detailing

Detailed reinforcement drawings, schedules, and bar bending schedules (BBS).

Steel Structure Design

Sheds, mezzanines, PEB, and industrial structures designed in STAAD.Pro.

Steel Detailing (Tekla)

Professional 3D detailing, GA, assembly & single-part drawings with Tekla Structures.

Quantity Estimation & BOQ

Accurate material take-off (MTO), BOQ, and cost estimation for RCC & steel.

Proposal & Tender Support

Professional proposal quotations, tender estimation, and bid documentation.

Shop & Fabrication Drawings

Detailed shop drawings and fabrication-ready documentation for steel structures.

PEB Engineering Support

Pre-engineered building design, detailing, and project coordination.
